Primary Documents in the History of American Political Thought

A companion collection to the American Political Thought reader by Keith E. Whittington.

This digital archive includes scores of freely available documents that excerpt primary source material in the history of American political thought. The collection features a wide range of materials, including books, pamphlets, speeches, and novels, that offer a window into the intellectual and political development of the United States.

Each excerpt is accompanied by an introductory header to provide essential context. This site is periodically updated and serves as a companion to the American Political Thought reader published by Oxford University Press.
