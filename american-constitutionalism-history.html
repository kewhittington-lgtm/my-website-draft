Primary Documents in the History of American Constitutionalism

A companion collection to American Constitutionalism by Howard Gillman, Mark A. Graber, and Keith E. Whittington.

This digital repository includes hundreds of freely available documents that excerpt primary source material in the history of American constitutionalism. The collection spans court cases, legislative hearings, executive branch legal opinions, and other vital documents that have shaped the American legal order.

Each excerpt includes an introductory header to provide historical and legal context. The site is periodically updated and serves as a companion to the American Constitutionalism casebooks published by Oxford University Press.
