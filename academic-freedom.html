Academic Freedom & Free Inquiry

Dedicated to the preservation of open inquiry, intellectual diversity, and the foundational principles of academic freedom within higher education. Through institutional leadership, campus outreach, and scholarly research, I work to ensure that the university remains a space for the free exchange of ideas.
